CFD of a Single Horizontal Axis Hydrokinetic Turbine

In this part of our research the flow field and performance characterization of two Horizontal Axis Hydrokinetic Turbines (HAHT) are presented. The first turbine is a laboratory scaled-model HAHT turbine.The second one is the full scale DOE Reference Model 1 (DOE RM1) turbine, the two bladed HAHT designed and published as a reference model by the department of energy.
