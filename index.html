About Me

Currently I am finishing my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering (ME), with focus on energy and fluids, at the University of Washington (UW). Prior to this I received my Masters of Science in ME from UW in 2010. I finished my Bachelor of Engineering in the AcUAS-SUT cooperation program between Sharif University of Technology in Iran and Aachen University of Applied Sciences in Germany. I am following two of my biggest passions, teaching and research, as an associate faculty member of the engineering department of Everett Community College and a part-time researcher at UW respectively.
